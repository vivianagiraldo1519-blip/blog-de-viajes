Where To Go

La Miel River

If you are looking for adventure, you must head out to La Miel River. It is a stunning natural paradise where you can try body rafting or tubing to float down the turquoise waters. While drifting, you might come across beautiful birds and lush vegetation along the banks.

Amaní Reservoir and Pantágoras Dam

To see the scale of the region's beauty, you must go over to the Amaní Reservoir. You can hop on a speedboat to explore the vast mirrors of water surrounded by tropical forests. Visitors should not miss the amazing view of the Pantágoras Dam.
